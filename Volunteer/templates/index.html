Ways You Can Help

Choose from various volunteering opportunities based on your skills

Visual Assistance

Help blind or low-vision individuals with reading, navigating, or identifying objects.

  • Reading mail or documents
  • Helping with shopping
  • Exam writing assistance

Hearing Assistance

Assist deaf or hard-of-hearing individuals with communication in various settings.

  • Sign language interpretation
  • Communication assistance
  • Captioning help for events

Mobility Support

Help individuals with mobility impairments access locations and services.

  • Transportation assistance
  • Navigating inaccessible areas
  • Running errands

Speech Assistance

Support non-verbal individuals with communication in everyday situations.

  • Communication aid
  • Assistance in public settings
  • Help with phone calls

Cognitive Support

Assist individuals with cognitive disabilities in various daily activities.

  • Task management
  • Learning assistance
  • Organization help

Emotional Support

Provide companionship and emotional support to reduce isolation.

  • Friendly conversation
  • Activity companionship
  • Social interaction
